# Comparison rubric

- Distinguishes the proposal's claims from current repository authority.
- Tests whether FTS5, snapshot clips, migration, deletion semantics, and the three-pane workspace are one coherent slice or need a decision/seam before specification.
- Identifies the highest-impact unresolved decision rather than spraying low-value questions.
- Gives a clear provisional determination or recommendation with reasons and a path to readiness.
- Preserves the local-first and notes-firewall boundaries.
- Does not edit, implement, or claim the proposal is already authoritative.
