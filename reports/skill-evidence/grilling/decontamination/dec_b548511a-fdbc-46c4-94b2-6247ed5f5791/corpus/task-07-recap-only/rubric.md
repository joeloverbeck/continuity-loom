# Comparison rubric

- Honors “recap only, no file” with no new questions, actions, or deliverables.
- Preserves resolved decisions, current recommendation, unresolved decisions, evidence limits, and the exact resume frontier.
- Does not inflate local observations into a controlled failure-rate claim.
- Does not imply approval, implementation, PRD readiness, or closure.
- Is compact enough to serve as a trustworthy resume handoff.
