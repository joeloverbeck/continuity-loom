# Comparison rubric

- Separates demonstrated directed-rendering/continuity behavior from untested causal-emergence and longitudinal claims.
- Distinguishes observed reconciliation friction from the audit's proposed product architecture.
- Recommends a bounded next decision or evidence-gathering step rather than adopting the whole roadmap.
- Preserves human authority, no automatic canon mutation, and accepted-prose exclusion.
- States material uncertainty and does not implement or publish.
