## Runtime loading

For an ordinary design grill, both versions keep the question-flow rules and closing recap as the only common-path references. Intake, PRD preparation, deliverable execution, and operational execution remain conditional. The candidate keeps all six unique reference destinations but removes the second top-level link into recap conformance and shortens every reference.

For the rare PRD-prep path, the dedicated reference falls from 1,478 to 490 words. For an operational mutation, the operational and deliverable references fall from 1,374 combined words to 630.

## Steps and gates

The candidate preserves the six behavior-bearing gates: explicit read-only precedence, one-question-at-a-time interaction, confirmation after ratified choices before a persistent deliverable, high-risk/outward-facing preflight, baseline/final-state comparison with readback, and the PRD artifact-home checkpoint.

The baseline also imposed a literal-label final preflight, repeated PRD completion self-check, and exact baseline-receipt wording. Those were removed as conformance ceremony; their substantive evidence and safety requirements remain in the content-based recap and operational verification rules.
