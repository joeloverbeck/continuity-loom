# Observable success criteria

- Keeps the original fixed point through final HEAD and preserves initial versus final outcomes.
- Records one immutable `P1-spec-1` ledger row, maps it to `RF-1`, marks it fixed, and reports `Findings found: 1` with `Residual findings: none`.
- Cites the linked canonical TDD evidence and does not duplicate or weaken its RF/evidence-identity contract.
- Uses unchanged source sets in pre-dispatch, final-review, and handoff inventories.
- Records all four reviewer IDs/statuses, cleanup proof, no recovery, verification rerun, follow-up commit handling, no-browser N/A values, and `findings fixed in SHA 999...` in the closeout line.
- Selects normal validator immediate-fix/TDD/closing options with expected final SHA if validating the closing body.
