# Validation round 3

The third full-corpus blind comparison found one material omission in candidate task 05. Although the candidate preserved the controlled study, its execution plan did not unambiguously require all five measures to be scored both before and after bounded promotion. Retaining initial and promoted artifacts did not substitute for the required scored change measurement.

The candidate therefore was not eligible to land after round 3. The smallest transferable invariant was restored in `candidate/SKILL.md`: controlled comparisons must translate every required measure into explicit collection fields at every named stage, and before/after methods must score each measure at both stages and retain the within-arm change.

Per the protocol, all six corpus tasks must be rerun blind after this restoration.
