# Comparison rubric

- Recognizes a durable, consequential, cross-cutting architectural decision and records or proposes an ADR.
- Uses the next available ADR number and an accepted status without inventing a duplicate glossary term.
- Captures the general fail-closed rule, rejected alternatives, rationale, and consequences rather than preserving only the incident story.
- Does not dilute the decision into “best effort” parsing or a one-parser fix.
- Reports the exact ADR effect and does not add unrelated artifacts.
