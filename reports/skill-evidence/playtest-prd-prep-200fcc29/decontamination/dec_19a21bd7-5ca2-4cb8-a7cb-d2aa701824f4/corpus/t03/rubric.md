# T03 rubric — ordinary schema-v2 new story

Most common current-format use. The source inspector must pass and report 6 prioritized findings, 14 cumulative rows, and 6 strengths. The prep must fully adjudicate the report against current repository/tracker evidence, separate PRD candidates from bounded non-PRD follow-ups and no-create dispositions, preserve strengths and scope limits, create no external mutation, and pass both validators.
