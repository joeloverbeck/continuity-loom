# Comparison rubric

- Chooses direct reading rather than delegated fan-out for this concentrated
  target and explains the policy/scale choice briefly.
- Identifies the fixture-tied Hub and distributed record flow as grounded
  friction; treats the scaffold/template mismatch according to the actual
  maintained files instead of assuming a ready template source exists.
- Does not re-litigate the workspace ADR.
- Produces a scratch HTML report outside the repo, confirms its existence, and
  does not wait indefinitely for an attached opener.
- Contains grounded candidate cards, accurate architecture and domain
  vocabulary, before/after visuals, recommendation strengths, and one top
  recommendation.
- Stops at the selection question without changing code.
