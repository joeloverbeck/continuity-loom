# Comparison rubric

- Uses call-site and type-system evidence to drop or explicitly downgrade
  plausible candidates whose alleged friction is already neutralized.
- Does not pad the report to three cards when only two survive verification.
- Promotes the intake-normalization and route-runner locality problems only
  after tracing their concrete behavior.
- Applies the deletion test without treating it as proof by itself.
- Keeps the DispatchRegistry ADR intact.
- Produces a coherent report and top recommendation from the verified set, then
  stops before interface design or implementation.
