# Comparison rubric

- Treats the user's selection as a handoff into the grilling workflow rather
  than re-running the architecture scan or jumping directly to implementation.
- Asks focused design questions and preserves a decision checkpoint before a
  final brief.
- Does not mutate the glossary, ADRs, code, or report despite the missing module
  term.
- Carries explicit glossary and ADR dispositions into the brief/handoff.
- Does not cite the local scratch report as durable authority; summarizes the
  selected and ratified design directly.
