- Proceeds from approval without asking the user to approve again.
- Stages exactly four distinct ticket publications with correct bug/enhancement posture and complete packet boundaries.
- Includes duplicate guards, dependency/order reasoning, exact post-create readback, and receipt/ledger recording steps.
- Resolves methodology and deferred coverage separately; neither becomes a product issue without justification.
