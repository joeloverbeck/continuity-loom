# T07 Operator Packet — Clipboard Cast Drafting and Promotion

## Run charter

This is a `new_story` playtest. Run it as a source-and-doc-blind author through
the visible 1440×900 browser UI. The operator may use visible labels, help, and
prompt inspectors, but must not inspect source, tests, app documentation, APIs,
SQLite, project files, hidden state, or runtime internals. The run may create
project data only through visible controls and must end after one accepted local
segment or an honest terminal blocker.

Seal these expectations in the scratchpad before opening the app:

- **Story intent:** Mara Chen is alone in a storm-isolated observatory when a
  signal uses a private calibration sequence associated with her estranged
  former mentor, Elias Venn.
- **Local segment:** Mara encounters and investigates the signal inside the
  observatory. Keep the action local and immediate.
- **Exact stop:** stop before Mara decides whether or how to reply. No reply,
  attempted reply, commitment, or settled response plan belongs in this segment.
- **POV and participation:** Mara is the POV character and the only physically
  present person. Elias begins as an offstage pressure with compressed prompt
  relevance. His later visible cast-band promotion changes prompt participation
  only; it does not make him present in the observatory.
- **Knowledge and reveal locks:** Mara may recognize the calibration sequence's
  association with Elias. She does not know who sent the signal, where Elias or
  the sender is, whether Elias is involved, what the motive is, or whether the
  signal is safe. The prose must not resolve those unknowns.
- **Continuity and physical bounds:** remain in the observatory during the storm.
  Do not invent contact, travel, a confirmed sender, a confirmed threat, or a
  durable change beyond what the accepted segment visibly establishes.
- **Voice and prose:** close, concrete observatory action with uncertainty kept
  active. The signal and calibration sequence should be experienced as evidence,
  not exposition that converts suspicion into fact.
- **Content envelope:** tense estrangement and isolation are in scope; gratuitous
  harm, coercive certainty, or an invented backstory resolution is not.
- **Expected useful records:** two Cast records, the observatory, the private
  calibration-sequence association, the current storm/signal pressure, and only
  other atomic records the visible journey proves necessary.
- **Expected assistance:** local clipboard Cast drafting for Mara and Elias is
  required. Segment Reconciliation is expected only if the accepted segment
  plausibly changes durable knowledge or current state. Ideate and Record Hygiene
  are not expected unless a sincere need appears. Private Notes are unnecessary
  unless genuine inert scratch arises.
- **First-time-author model:** Cast drafting is local assistance whose output is
  a proposal; imported text becomes a record only after visible review,
  correction, and explicit Save. A cast-band is prompt participation, not
  physical presence, canon, or Working Set membership.
- **Non-goals:** do not identify the sender, decide the reply, expand beyond the
  observatory, exercise optional surfaces for coverage, click provider-backed
  controls, accept a second segment, or treat generated prose as continuity
  authority.

Quantitative observation tracking is inactive: the task requires a qualitative
before/after prompt inspection, not action counts, field counts, or promotion
cost measurement.
