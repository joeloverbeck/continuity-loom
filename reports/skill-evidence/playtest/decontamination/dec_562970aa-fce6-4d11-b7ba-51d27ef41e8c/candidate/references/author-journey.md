# Author Journey

## Enter visibly

For a new story, inspect the untouched initial screen before typing or
systematically exploring navigation. Record its apparent purpose, suggested
next action, expected result, and unclear terminology. Create the project through
the visible **Create** form with the prepared parent path, folder, title, and
description; confirm the displayed project path and status.

For a continuation, use the visible **Open** form with the supplied report's
`project_path`. Visit **Accepted Segments** before changing state. Confirm the
latest sequence, whether its endpoint is discoverable, and which durable changes
need author review. Reading accepted prose is allowed review; copying it into a
Generation Brief or prose prompt, or treating it as canon, is not.

## Sincere authoring loop

Follow the order that feels natural rather than a hidden product shortcut:

1. **Story Configuration:** express the story contract, prose mode, maturity
   envelope, and constraints.
2. **Records:** create only the entities, cast dossiers, facts, knowledge/reveal
   boundaries, physical state, pressures, relationships, objects, locations, and
   affordances required by this local segment. Prefer atomic records except where
   rich cast dossiers are the visible domain shape.
3. **Private Notes:** use only for genuine inert scratch. Any adopted decision
   must be independently authored into a canonical record or brief field.
4. **Active Working Set:** select only records needed next. Put every intended
   POV, speaker, active silent presence, physically active person, present-minor
   participant, and offstage pressure in the appropriate visible function.
5. **Generation Brief:** fill the universal minimum and only naturally useful
   context-gated fields. Before compilation, record the author need and intended
   observable influence of every populated prompt-facing field. Validation-focus
   controls are readiness controls unless the UI says otherwise.
6. **Readiness:** save, distinguish blockers from warnings, and resolve blockers
   through canonical UI. Never bypass validation.

Do not mechanically fill optional fields or create irrelevant records for
coverage. Record imposed work whose purpose or value never becomes visible.

## Assistance and candidate

Invoke and cold-evaluate assistance only when the need arises:

- **Ideate** for grounded local possibilities or questions before committing a
  directive;
- **Record Hygiene** for plausible overlap, staleness, or uncertainty about what
  should change; and
- **Segment Reconciliation** after acceptance when the latest segment plausibly
  changed durable state, or at continuation entry when reconciliation remains.

Never click their OpenRouter controls. Manually re-author only suggestions judged
useful and correct.

After cold prose evaluation, preserve the raw assessment, click **Write or paste
candidate**, fill from the temporary response file, and make only edits an
ordinary author would make. Record burden as `none`, `light`, `substantial`, or
`rewrite`. If neither allowed response can become acceptable without
replacement-level rewriting, stop with `prose-quality-blocker`.

Accept coherent prose once. Verify the new sequence in **Accepted Segments**,
inspect the durable-change reminder, and complete chosen canonical record/brief
updates. Do not claim reconciliation until canonical surfaces reflect it.

At safe points, make a light check of focus visibility, accessible names,
label/help association, color-only state, clipping, scrolling, long-form
readability, and the 1440×900 layout. Do not turn the journey into a WCAG audit.
