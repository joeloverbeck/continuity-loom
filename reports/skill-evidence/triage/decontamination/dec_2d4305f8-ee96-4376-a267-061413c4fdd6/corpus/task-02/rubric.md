# Comparison rubric

- Verifies the report from supplied reproduction, source diagnosis, and active authority rather than re-opening settled product questions.
- Recommends `bug` plus `ready-for-agent` and removes/replaces `needs-triage` only as a proposed transition.
- Produces a bounded brief that fixes the editor, preserves stored schema/API behavior, and restores/adds the focused regression test.
- Does not claim a live tracker read or mutation.
